## FAQ

### Q: Can I still see overall rankings?
**A:** Yes! Switch to "Custom Filter" tab. The overall rankings are still calculated and available.

### Q: How many 1st place winners will there be?
**A:** Depends on combinations. If you have:
- 7 categories
- 2 variety options per category (None + one variety)
- 4 age divisions
- 3 ability levels

Maximum possible: 7 Ã— 2 Ã— 4 Ã— 3 = 168 groups

In practice, you'll have far fewer because not every combination will have entries.

### Q: Does this affect the Medal Program?
**A:** Currently, Medal Program still uses overall rankings for point awards. This may be updated in the future to use category combination rankings.

### Q: What if a dancer is the only one in their combination?
**A:** They'll be 1st place in that combination! They still earned it by participating.

### Q: Can I export just one combination?
**A:** Not yet, but this is a planned feature. Currently, exports include all entries with both overall and category combination ranks.

## TIPS

### For Competition Organizers:

1. **Review Combinations Before Competition**
   - Check how many entries per combination
   - Consider combining sparse combinations if needed

   - Explain the new ranking system
   - Clarify that "1st Place" means "1st in their category combination"

3. **Use Appropriate View Mode**
   - "By Category Combination" for awards ceremonies
   - "Custom Filter" for specific analysis

### For Judges:

- No changes to scoring process
- Score entries normally
- Ranking calculations happen automatically

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### Problem: "I don't see any groups"
**Solution:** Judging may not be complete. Wait for all judges to submit scores.

### Problem: "Too many small groups"
**Solution:** This is normal if you have diverse entries. Consider this means you have good category coverage!

### Problem: "I want the old view back"
**Solution:** Click "Custom Filter" tab. The original view is still there!

### Problem: "Export doesn't show category ranks"
**Solution:** Make sure you've re-exported after this update. Old exports won't have the new columns.
